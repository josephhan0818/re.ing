About re-ing

re-ing is a forward-thinking brand based in Rotterdam, the Netherlands, dedicated to promoting sustainable product design services and serving as a trusted material agency for Taiwanese eco-materials. As a subsidiary of Dot Design Co., Ltd. (點睛設計有限公司), we bridge cross-cultural innovation and sustainability with purpose.

Our Services

1. Sustainable Design Services:
We help brands and businesses create eco-conscious products that align with circular economy principles, from concept development to prototyping and material sourcing.

2. Taiwan Material Agency:
As your connection to Taiwan’s cutting-edge sustainable materials, we represent innovative suppliers and promote responsible material usage in European markets.
