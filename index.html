About re-ing

re-ing is a design-driven initiative based in Rotterdam, promoting circular thinking and sustainable product innovation. Backed by DOT Design Co., we connect Taiwan’s cutting-edge material partners with European sustainability markets.

Our Services

Material Agency We represent sustainable suppliers across Taiwan, connecting European designers with innovative biomaterials and recycled resources.
Sustainable Product Design We collaborate with brands to co-create eco-conscious products using circular principles—from concept to prototype.
